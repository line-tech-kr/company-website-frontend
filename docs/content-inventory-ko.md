### Community thread bodies (extracted)

**wr_id=6 (Views: 8,387):** O-ring sealing material depends on gas; check catalogue for sealing per gas. If desired gas shares same sealant material as owned MFC, user may apply a conversion factor. Warning verbatim: "만일 sealing 이 다를경우 절대 사용하시면 안됩니다" (if sealing differs, absolutely do not use).

**wr_id=5:** Sensor contains two temperature sensors in parallel (negative/positive sides). If negative sensor fails to register, positive sensor saturates to max output. "제조사에 문의하시기바랍니다" (contact manufacturer).

**wr_id=4 (Views: 6,651):** MFC operates with three fixed inputs — temperature, pressure, flow. If operating pressure drifts significantly high or low from calibration, or temperature changes materially, flow output deviates.

**wr_id=3:** Three causes: (1) zero-point drift — "0 점은 현장에서 유저가 직접 조절가능합니다" (user can zero on site); (2) line leak / valve-assembly defect — contact manufacturer; (3) electrical noise via cables and controller.

**wr_id=2:** Usually one of two causes: "주로 Sensor 내부 오작동이거나. Cable 결선이 잘못 되었을시 발생됩니다" (internal sensor malfunction or cable miswire).

**wr_id=1 (Views: 2,647):** Check (1) gas cylinder valve open & gas remaining, (2) cable wiring between controller and MFC correct, (3) MFC outlet not blocked/valved off. If none, likely an MFC defect.

All six threads have 0 replies.
